JMR30 COVID Policy

Menschwork’s Wisdom Council has adopted the following COVID-19 policy applicable to JMR30. This policy is subject to change as the COVID-19 situation, State of Connecticut requirements, or facility policies require. Please click this link to access the State of Connecticut’s COVID guidance website.

Pre-retreat Requirements

Each retreat attendee is required to either:

  1. be fully vaccinated as of September 30, 2021, or
  2. have a negative COVID-19 test seventy-two (72) hours prior to arrival with the expectation that the attendee will self-isolate until you arrive on campus

All retreat attendees will be asked to sign a waiver and provide confirmation regarding no exposure. recent travel & good health along with COVID-19 practices to keep us safe. Please be prepared to show proof of being fully vaccinated by September 30, 2021 or a negative COVID-19 test within 72 hours of the retreat.

Retreat guidelines

We ask that everyone acknowledge a set of guidelines, which may include individuals identifying one’s desire to maintain social distance, overall social distance guidelines and wearing of masks in large and small group settings as required and/or requested.

Prayer and Program Spaces:

Indoor, non-boarding spaces will be used for small gatherings and will have a max capacity based on COVID guidelines at the time. We will gather in large indoor spaces as deemed appropriate based on those same guidelines and as weather dictates. The facility has added a large (60’ x 120’) tent which will be used to accommodate larger group gatherings, as appropriate.

Masks:

All men must honor requests to wear a mask during indoor, non-socially distanced gatherings should they occur.

Updates to these guidelines

Specific protocols will be drawn up, and shared with the community, that support federal, state and local guidelines as we get closer to the retreat

Refunds

We understand that this overall situation may change, as well as your personal situation. Refunds will be honored, minus a $50 processing fee, for cancellations no later than September 30, 2021.
